Services

What we deliver

We specialise in implementing business-critical platforms, configured to your requirements, governed properly, and adopted by your teams.

Microsoft 365

SharePoint Online & Microsoft 365 Implementation

We design and build SharePoint Online intranets, hub sites, and collaboration environments that meet enterprise governance standards while remaining genuinely usable.

Our approach covers information architecture, navigation design, permissions governance, content migration, and user adoption. Everything is geared towards making your investment in Microsoft 365 deliver measurable value.

  • Intranet and hub site design and build
  • Information architecture and taxonomy
  • Governance frameworks and permissions models
  • Content migration from legacy platforms
  • Teams and collaboration workspace strategy
  • Power Platform integration where appropriate
  • User adoption and change management
CRM

CRM Implementation

We implement CRM platforms that give your client-facing teams a single, reliable view of relationships, pipeline, and activity, configured around how your business actually works.

Platform-agnostic and pragmatic, we focus on the fundamentals: clean data, sensible processes, and an interface people will actually use every day.

  • Requirements analysis and platform selection support
  • System configuration and customisation
  • Data cleansing, mapping, and migration
  • Integration with email, calendar, and core business systems
  • Reporting and dashboard design
  • User training and adoption programmes
ITSM

Service Desk Implementation

We implement service desk and ITSM platforms that reflect your actual support processes, not a generic out-of-the-box configuration that nobody follows.

Whether you need a straightforward IT helpdesk or a full ITIL-aligned service management capability, we configure it to work within your operational reality.

  • Service desk platform configuration
  • Incident, request, change, and problem management workflows
  • Self-service portal design
  • SLA configuration and escalation rules
  • Knowledge base setup and structure
  • Integration with monitoring, asset management, and identity systems
  • Reporting on service performance metrics
Integration

Systems Integration

Most organisations run dozens of platforms that don't talk to each other. We connect them. We build the integrations, data flows, and automation that turn a collection of tools into a joined-up system.

Whether it's syncing your CRM with your finance platform, connecting your service desk to monitoring tools, or building custom API integrations, we make your systems work together so your people don't have to be the glue.

  • API and middleware integration design and build
  • Data mapping, transformation, and synchronisation
  • Platform connector configuration
  • Workflow automation across systems
  • Integration testing and monitoring
  • Legacy system bridging and migration support

About

Founder-led, senior-delivered

Implify Solutions is a consultancy built on the principle that implementation work should be done by people who care about getting it right.

Implify Solutions Ltd was founded on a simple idea: implement and simplify. Too much of IT consulting is unnecessarily painful. Overengineered processes, impenetrable jargon, and a strange reluctance to just get on with the work.

We're a founder-led consultancy that works with organisations who need technology implementations done properly. Microsoft 365, CRM, service desks, systems integration. We take complex platforms and make them work in the real world.

We're straightforward, easy to work with, and genuinely invested in your success. The consultant you engage is the person who does the work. We fail fast, communicate openly, and never lose sight of the fact that you're the client.

No theatre. No jargon for the sake of it. Just experienced people who care about doing good work and are actually pleasant to have around while they do it.
